Jordan Laurenti also known as Jbay is a singer / songwriter from New York. He has performed up and down the east coast throughout his music career and has since been performing at various venues throughout Florida. His sound is delicate and soulful, his voice adds a creative touch to any song he sings. Jordan sings a variety of cover songs ranging from pop, indie, reggae, folk & rock, but also throws in many original songs as well. The set at each performance is carefully put together so that each gig can be special and genuine. Jordan recently opened up for two national touring acts, Mayday and The Nappy roots. Jordan has been featured on videos for Balcony tv , soflo music school and A song Catcher project . You can catch Jordan weekly at a number of venues in the West Palm Beach / Southern Florida area, as well as NYC and Long Island.
